## Key Enhancements

### Market Structure

The primary prediction market question is:
- **"Will BrAIdog be enhanced with capabilities to speak human language?"**

This is a clear, binary question that resolves based on whether BrAIdog can actually produce recognizable human words.

Additional related markets may appear throughout the timeline:
- Hour 4: "Will BrAIdog be confirmed as a language model host?" (more technical/specific)
- Hour 7: "Will BrAIdog successfully pronounce a recognized human word on stream?" (final test market)

### Outcome System

The story has four possible outcomes:
- **Outcome A - Strong YES**: Dog speaks clearly as human
- **Outcome B - Weak YES**: Dog said "Hello" weakly
- **Outcome C - Weak NO**: Tried to speak but failed
- **Outcome D - Strong NO**: Total hoax exposed

Balanced system: 2 YES outcomes, 2 NO outcomes.
